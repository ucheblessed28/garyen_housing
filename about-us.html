About us

Garyen Enterprises

We Value Our Customers, Therefore Providing Valuable Business.

Garyen is a home and neighbourhood site for buyers, sellers and renters to find homes and properties across Nigeria through recommendations, local insights and map overlay that offer details on commute, reported crimes, schools churches and nearby businesses.

Garyen offers a variety of Real estate and neighborhood products and services to consumers and realtors. We provide price trend information by using listing and public data which includes how the price of a home has home has changed over a period of time.

At Garyen Enterprises, we send qualified leads - prospective renters and buyers - to advertisers to help them maximize the return on investment of their funds. We also facilitate our visitors to get rent & purchases estimates (G-estimates) of properties, and also enable them to find properties and detailed information about these properties.
